dvd ripping

30 Sep 2007

**Rip audio from a dvd **

mencoder dvd://2 -oac mp3lame -lameopts br=96:cbr:vol=6 -ovc frameno -o track2.mp3

for i in ../vobs/* ; do j=${i##*/} mencoder "${i}" -oac pcm -ovc frameno -o "${j}" done
