Salary vs Contract Rates

19 Jan 2010
I’ve always wondered what recruitment agents use as their formula to convert between salaries and contract rates. Here’s a formula (for giving ballpark figures) – thanks CK: Salary divided by 48 weeks, divided by 40 hours, plus 20% (super, training, sick leave, etc) =~ Hourly rate For example, $100K =~ $62.50/hour, $150K =~ $93.75/hour. Another year, another Sydney Film Festival

Another year, another Sydney Film Festival. Two films I saw really stood out – The Cove (about the continued Japanese dolphin slaughter) and Che Part I and Part II (covering the Cuban and Bolivian years of Ernesto ‘Che’ Guevara).
